About Me

Hello! I'm Hriday Harlalka, a passionate and dedicated Data Scientist with over 3 years of experience specializing in Data Analytics and Machine Learning Modelling. Based in Atlanta, I've developed a strong foundation in ML and AI technologies, especially NLP and Computer Vision, aiming to leverage my skills to contribute to complex data analysis pipleines.

Educational Background:

I hold a Masters in Analytics from Georgia Institute of Technology, where I honed my skills in Deep Learning, Computer Vision and Statistical Analysis.

Professional Journey:

My career has taken me through diverse roles, from Businiess Intelligence at Piramal Capital and Housing Finance to Data Scientist at Reliance Jio. This journey has equipped me with a broad set of skills and a unique perspective on various data complexities and theor respective solutions.
